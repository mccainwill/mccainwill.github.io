Design Projects

These are things I have designed and built either wholly or in part. Some are case studies and collaborations, while others are freelance or personal work.

Marvel BattleApp

Case Study

As part of the Iron Yard, I was one of two designers paired up with developers to make a web app using Marvel's API of character data. The user would be able to select any hero or villian, see their stats and description, and then pit them in battle against any other character. Design-wise, we went with flat, vibrant colors and powerful, energetic fonts reminiscent of comic books.

Center for Fathers and Families

Github Site

Another Iron Yard project, this was a redesign of an old version of the Center's website. Since its aim is to help struggling fathers with the information and support they need, I decided on soft, comfortable colors and structured the content to better find resources.
